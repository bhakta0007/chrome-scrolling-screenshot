# Privacy Policy for Scrollable Screenshot Extension

**Last Updated:** [Current Date]

## Information Collection

Scrollable Screenshot is a privacy-focused browser extension that operates entirely on your local device. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about data handling.

### What We Collect

**We do NOT collect any personal information or usage data.**

Scrollable Screenshot operates completely offline and processes all screenshots locally within your browser. No data is transmitted to external servers.

### What We Don't Collect

- No personal information
- No browsing history
- No usage analytics
- No user identifiers
- No screenshot data
- No website information

### How the Extension Works

1. **Local Processing**: All screenshot capturing and processing happens locally in your browser
2. **No Server Communication**: The extension does not communicate with any external servers
3. **No Data Storage**: Screenshots are not stored by the extension - they are downloaded directly to your device
4. **No Tracking**: We do not track your usage or behavior

### Permissions Explained

Scrollable Screenshot requests the following minimal permissions:

- **activeTab**: Access the currently active tab to enable screenshot functionality
- **scripting**: Inject content scripts to create the selection overlay
- **downloads**: Enable downloading of captured screenshots to your device

These permissions are strictly used for the core functionality of taking screenshots and are not used for any other purpose.

### Data Security

Since no data is collected or transmitted, there are no data security concerns. All processing happens locally within your browser sandbox.
